# Problem Formulation – Analyzing_NYC_High_School_Data

## Research / business question

Do AP participation and outcomes differ by borough and demographic after controlling for available confounders, and which schools or boroughs should be prioritized for interventions?

## Primary success metric and threshold

- **Correlation and descriptive stats** by borough and demographic; effect-size measures and simple regressions where appropriate.
- **Baseline:** Borough or sample averages for comparison.

## Stakeholders and decisions

- **Educators / policy:** Decisions about funding and intervention targeting. **Product vision:** NYC school insights platform (dashboard, export for policy).
