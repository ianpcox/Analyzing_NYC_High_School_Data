# Data Sheet – Analyzing_NYC_High_School_Data

## Data sources

- **Primary:** NYC high school CSVs (sat_results, demographics, ap_2010, graduation, class_size, hs_directory, survey files). Document source (e.g. NYC Open Data), version/date, and license.
- **Splits:** EDA and inference; no train/test unless building a predictive model. Document limitations (missing values, coverage by borough).
- **Demographics and biases:** Student demographics (race, gender); document collection and known selection biases.
